About me

Hi, I am Ali Qureshi. I am a UX/UI designer with 7 years of experience in Graphic Design. I specialize in UX/UI Design, Graphic & Web Design and enjoy solving real-life problems. I approach my design-thinking with the double-diamond process; Phase 1: Research (Design the right thing), Phase 2: Design (Design things right)
